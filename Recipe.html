Paella

A traditional Spanish dish from Valencia, originally a simple meal for farm workers. Today, it is a celebrated dish with various regional variations.

Serving Size 4 servings
Prep Time 30 minutes
Cook Time 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 500g seafood (shrimp, mussels, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp saffron
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

  • Paella pan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in the paella pan over medium heat.
  2. Fry garlic, onion, and bell pepper until soft.
  3. Add rice and saffron, then pour in the broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add seafood and cook until tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.

Fideuà

A delicious Spanish dish from Valencia, similar to paella but made with noodles instead of rice. It is typically made with seafood and a rich, flavorful broth.

Serving Size 4 servings
Prep Time 20 minutes
Cook Time 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g fideuà noodles
  • 500g seafood (shrimp, mussels, squid)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tomato, grated
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 500ml seafood broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

  • Fideuà pan
  • Wooden spoon

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in the pan and sauté onion, garlic, and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add the grated tomato and paprika, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the seafood and cook until slightly browned.
  4. Pour in the seafood broth and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add noodles and cook until the broth is absorbed and the noodles are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.

Tortilla de Patatas

A classic Spanish omelette made with eggs, potatoes, and onions. It's a simple yet delicious dish that's perfect for any meal.

Serving Size 4 servings
Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

  • Non-stick frying pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and sauté onions and potatoes until tender.
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Once the potatoes and onions are cooked, mix them with the eggs.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the pan and cook on low heat until the bottom is set.
  5. Flip the tortilla using a plate and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot or cold, cut into wedges.
